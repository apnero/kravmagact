Our Team

Chris Petitti

Chris Petitti brings more than 13 years of experience in the field of executive protection and advanced security operations to Cannon Ridge Training Center. Mr. Petitti has provided security coverage for many well-known celebrities, as well as corporate executives and former government officials. He is Certified through the Israeli Krav Maga Association (Gidon System) as well as Nir Maman’s CT707 Israeli Special Forces Krav Maga. Through years of executive protection and Krav Maga Street Defense experience, Chris brings real life experiences and street scenarios to his students.

His credentials include:

  • IKMA Certified Krav Maga Instructor
  • CT707 Certified Israeli Special Forces Krav Maga Instructor
  • Executive Protection
  • CPR, First Aid & AED
  • Security Operations Management
  • Firearms Training
  • Counter Terrorism

Ron Osborne

Ron is a former high school and collegiate football player who has always been passionate about training. Over the last two years, he has been implementing his style of conditioning bootcamp throughout Fairfield county. Ron employs a positive reinforcement approach to fitness which keeps his students motivated from the time they walk through the door until the last set is complete.

Chris Berlin

Chris is a life long martial artist and personal trainer. He has studied Kickboxing, Boxing, Kung Fu, Muay Thai, and brings the martial arts principles to working with and teaching clients. His personal instruction uses techniques borrowed from centuries of martial arts proven to work with athletes of a wide array of sports as well as people just looking to start getting in shape.
